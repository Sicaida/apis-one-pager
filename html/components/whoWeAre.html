Who we are

Apis specialises in the business of government. Our primary purpose is to assist public service leaders in the effective delivery of their business. But we pride ourselves on brining a lot more to the relationship. We are a firm of practitioners, known for collaboratively shaping and solving major initiatives, together with our government clients.


What's with the bull?

The Apis Bull has been with us since our foundation in 1999. It is synonymous with our name. Its origins are based on Apis, the bull god, symbolising prosperity and foresight and providing trusted guidance on issues of change.
