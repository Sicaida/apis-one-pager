Delivery

At Apis, our primary focus is program/project design and integration: the planning, management, delivery and assurance of complex policy reform initiatives, combining the key elements of capability (people, process, technology) and transforming them into tangible outcomes.

Our delivery management services include business and ICT project and program management, reform implementation, agile delivery and transformation management. Our work is underpinned by industry best practices and we apply our services across the full delivery lifecycle, from concept to closure and benefits realisation.

Examples of our delivery management services include:

  • Hands-on program and project management
  • Project Management Office (PMO) design, implementation and ongoing
    • management
  • Development of tailored project management frameworks, processes, tools and
    • templates
  • Developing and managing integrated plans and schedules
  • Monitoring, reviewing and evaluating progress and outcomes
  • Release management
  • Risks and issues management
  • Resource management
  • Benefits planning and management
  • Transformation planning and management, including change impact assessments
  • Developing and delivering tailored communications and stakeholder engagement
