At Apis, our primary focus is program/project design and integration: the planning, management, delivery and assurance of complex policy reform initiatives, combining the key elements of capability (people, process, technology) and transforming them into tangible outcomes.

Our delivery management services include business and ICT project and program management, reform implementation, agile delivery and transformation management. Our work is underpinned by industry best practices and we apply our services across the full delivery lifecycle, from concept to closure and benefits realisation.

Apis offers services to design, plan and manage complex procurement, through sourcing strategy development, tender management, contract negotiation, contract management and transition or decommissioning. We do this across business services, ICT services, software, facilities, infrastructure, grants and funded programs. We possess expertise in commissioning and market engagement.
