Advisory

Apis offers a range of advisory services to clients. We do this across two primary streams of services offering:

Strategic Advice: Strategic planning and analysis of organisations, government programs, and policy initiatives that contribute to transformation in some way. This work includes:

  • Designing strategies for implementing new policy proposals, concept and feasibility analysis
  • Developing business cases and investment proposals
  • Financial modelling and cost / price analyses
  • Program implementation planning
  • Organisational capability improvement
  • Programme planning including governance and risk management
  • Workload (demand) and workforce (supply) analysis, modelling and management
  • Data Analytics including statistical and demographic analysis

Evaluation and Review: Proactive assurance and evaluation of organisations, government programs and transformation programmes and projects. This work includes:

  • Programme and project evaluations and assurance reviews
  • Compliance reviews
  • Organisational performance reviews
  • Risk reviews
  • Developing assurance frameworks

We offer services to public and private sector organisations, formt he smallest to the largest. Within government, we have supported policy departments, service provision agencies, regulators and central agencies, across multiple sectors. We work on a stand alone basis, or equally within an existing program or alongside multi-disciplinary teams.
