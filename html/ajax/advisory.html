Advisory

Apis supports organisations with defining business outcomes and capabilities and developing strategies and roadmaps to deliver on agreed commitments. This spans operational and strategic planning, business case development, portfolio / program design, blueprinting, enterprise risk management, stakeholder engagement, benefits and change management.

Apis conducts independent reviews (Health Checks) of programmes, projects and business systems, with the objective of making practical and sustainable recommendations for improvement. Apis has extensive experience in clarifying programme / project objectives, reviewing artefacts, determining areas of risk, identifying issues against key performance indicators and undertaking the end-to-end process of review (design of review approach, review planning, undertaking the review, development of recommendations and capture of lessons learned).
