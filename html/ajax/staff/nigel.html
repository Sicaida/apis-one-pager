Nigel Nutt

Nigel is a partner and director of the Apis Group, with responsibility and oversight across all aspects of the successful operations of the business.

He joined the firm in 2002 and since then has led numerous consulting assignments for a wide range of government and private sector clients. His skills lie in the disciplines of project management, program evaluation, complex procurement, strategic consulting and analytical problem solving. He has advised on numerous major reform and corporate improvement initiatives for government across many sectors including health, human services, social services, customs and border security and international aid among others.
