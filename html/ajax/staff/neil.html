Neil Mann

X

As a Principal Consultant with Apis, Neil specialises in strategic planning, program management and business process design services within the health, aged care, education, biosecurity and environment sectors. Neil was heavily involved in the business case development for the Aged Care Gateway known as My Aged Care. He led the development of requirements for a new national aged care assessment workforce, including training and support tools. More recently Neil led the development and implementation of the My Age Care monitoring and evaluation strategy and conducted a review of how well My Aged Care is supporting clients in rural and remote Australia.
