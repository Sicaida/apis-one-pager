Nidal
Adhami

Nidal has been part of the Apis team since 2010, and is a senior consultant with strong experience in complex government procurement and contracting, as well as programme, project and risk management. Working with the Department of Social Services, Nidal managed the end-to-end procurement process for a national Regional Assessment Service (RAS) for the Aged Care Gateway (ACG) Programme. This led to the establishment of contracts with 13 providers at a value of $210 million, to deliver home support assessment services nationally. In this role Nidal managed a team delivering the logistics of the procurement, delivered approach to market documents and evaluation training, and managed schedule and risk to deliver executed contracts ahead of schedule.
