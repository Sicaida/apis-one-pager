Who
We
Are

Apis specialises in the business of government. Our primary purpose is to assist public service leaders in the effective delivery of their business. But we pride ourselves on brining a lot more to the relationship. We are a firm of practitioners, known for collaboratively shaping and solving major initiatives, together with our government clients.


What's with the bull?

The Apis Bull has been with us since our foundation in 1999. It is synonymous with our name. Its origins are based on Apis, the bull god, symbolising prosperity and foresight and providing trusted guidance on issues of change.

A few of our People

Apis specialises in strategic advisory services, hands-on delivery management and business and digital design.

community

Supporting the local community is a fundamental part of Apis’ business. The Apis in the Community Program allows us to make a significant contribution to community based programs, events and charities aimed at helping those in need. We do this through volunteer work, participation in community events and through fundraising.

Charity of Choice

In 2017-18 Apis has selected Lifeline as our charity of choice. We are involved in a range of fundraising and community events with Lifeline Canberra with our objective to assist Lifeline to fund the training of more volunteer crisis support staff to man their 24 hour phone service.
