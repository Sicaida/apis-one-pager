Who We Are

Apis specialises in the Business of government. Our primary purpose is to assist public service leaders in the effective delivery of government business. But we pride ourselves on brining a lot more to the relationship. We are a firm of practitioners, known for cooperatively shaping and solving major initiatives, together with our government clients.


What's with the bull?

The Apis Bull has been with us since our foundation in 1999. It is synonymous with our name – its origins were based on Apis, the bull god, symbolising prosperity and foresight and providing trusted guidance on issues of change.

What We Do

Apis specialises in strategic advisory and digital transformation services as well as hands-on solutions in delivery management, business analysis and design, digital design, change and transformation, complex procurement and program evaluation and review.

Advisory

Apis offers a range of advisory services to clients. We do this across two primary streams of services.

Design

Full business and digital design services from user research and service design.

Delivery

Planning, management, delivery and assurance of complex policy reform initiatives.

Who We help

Community

Supporting the local community is a fundamental part of Apis’ business. The Apis in the Community Program allows us to make a significant contribution to community based programs, events and charities aimed at helping those in need. We do this through volunteer work, participation in community events and through fundraising.

Charity of Choice

In 2017-18 Apis has selected Lifeline as our charity of choice. We are involved in a range of fundraising and community events with Lifeline Canberra with our objective to assist Lifeline to fund the training of more volunteer crisis support staff to man their 24 hour phone service.
