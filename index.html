Who We Are

Apis is a Canberra based professional services firm specialising in strategic advisory and digital transformation services as well as hands-on solutions in delivery management, business analysis, digital design, complex procurement and program evaluation and review. We are a firm of practitioners, known for cooperatively shaping and solving major initiatives, together with our government clients.

Our purpose is to become partners and trusted advisors to our clients; to inspire our people to achieve their best in interesting and challenging environments; and to be pragmatic and agile in meeting our clients’ challenges.

Community

Supporting the local community is a fundamental part of Apis’ business. The Apis in the Community Program allows us to make a significant contribution to community based programs, events and charities aimed at helping those in need.

We do this through volunteer work, participation in community events and through fundraising.

In 2017-18 Apis has selected Lifeline as our charity of choice. We are involved in a range of fundraising and community events with Lifeline Canberra with our objective to assist Lifeline to fund the training of more volunteer crisis support staff to man their 24 hour phone service.
